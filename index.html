About Us

This is where it comes true!

Welcome to Aetheria, one of the largest community driven VR cities ever created. Our district will be the premiere cyberpunk destination on Decentraland, a social VR platform built on the blockchain. Aetheria will be filled with a variety of attractions to entertain and challenge both visitors and regular users.

Aetheria exists as a not-for-profit organisation within Decentraland with the goal of realizing cyberpunk ethos and aesthetic. The project is largely community developed and driven, with a District Leadership team serving to lead the project in the interim, until an Aragon based DAO can be established, and a board elected by the Aetherian community.
